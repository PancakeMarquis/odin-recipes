Tortas de Jamon

Description

A torta con jamon is a classic mexicain dish consisting of a special type of bread call un Bolio, jamon or ham, cheese and vegtables like lettece and tomatos.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Mayo
  • Cheese
  • Lettece
  • Tomato
  1. First cut the head in half
  2. Toast both sides
  3. Apply mayo on both sides
  4. Add ham to bottom side
  5. Add cheese, lettece, tomato on top
  6. Put top of bread on
