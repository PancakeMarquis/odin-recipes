Nachos

Description

Fried torillas covered in melty cheese topped with pico de gallo, jalapenos and carne asada.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Carne Asada
  • Jalepenos
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Add chips to a sheet pan
  2. Scatter cheese over them
  3. Cook in oven till cheese melts
  4. Cut up tomato,onion and cilantro to make pico de gallo
  5. Add jalepenos, carne asada y pico de gallo
