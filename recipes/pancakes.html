Pancakes

Description

Tasty homemade chocolate chip pancakes. Very easy to make and quick.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Sugar
  • Buttermilk
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips
  1. Mix the flour, sugar and baking soda
  2. Add the buttermilk
  3. Mix until slightly lumpy
  4. Heat up pan and add butter
  5. Add batter
  6. Pour in batter
  7. Add chocolate chips
  8. When bubbly filp
  9. Remove when done
  10. Let cool before eating
